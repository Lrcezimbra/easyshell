# Working with files

Where am I?

`$ pwd`

Moving things around 

`$ mv -v <filename> /another/path/`

Copying stuff

`$ cp -v <filename> <another-filename>`

Deleting files

`$ rm -v <filename>`

Deleting folders

`$ rm -vR /path/to/<folder>`

Updating timestamp

`$ touch <filename>`

Listing files and directories

`$ ls -al`

# Remember!

## Flag **-v** is for verbosity.
